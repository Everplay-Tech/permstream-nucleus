# PermStream Nucleus: General Pricing Plan

PermStream Nucleus utilizes an **Asymmetric Open Source** model to balance viral ecosystem distribution with sustainable IP protection.

---

## ⚖️ 1. The Licensing Framework

### **A. Core Decoder (FOSS)**
*   **Components:** `libpermstream`, `psfsd` (decode paths), and all integrations.
*   **License:** **Apache 2.0**
*   **Philosophy:** Ensure that PermStream-encoded data can be read, searched, and integrated into any project (DuckDB, DataFusion, PyTorch) for free, eliminating "read-lock" friction.

### **B. Enterprise Encoder (Proprietary)**
*   **Components:** `nucleus-writer` (High-efficiency AI Predictor & Braid Optimizer).
*   **License:** **Business Source License (BSL 1.1)**
*   **Additional Use Grant:** 
    *   **Free:** Personal use, hobbyist projects, and businesses with **<$5M USD** annual revenue.
    *   **Paid:** Commercial use by large enterprises or managed service providers.
*   **Change Date:** Converts to Apache 2.0 on March 14, 2030.

## 🛡️ 3. IP Sovereignty (2026 Strategy)
The BSL license protects the **A3B Predictor Weights** and **Braid Topology Math**. These represent years of R&D in neural data engineering. By distributing the encoder as a hardened Rust binary, we ensure that:
1.  Hyperscalers cannot fork the project to offer "PermStream-as-a-Service" without contributing to the project's sustainability.
2.  Senior architects can trust the "Free Reader" for their long-term data accessibility.
